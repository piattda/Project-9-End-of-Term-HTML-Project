How do I become a professional, accredited, and
successful Interpreter?

Meet David!

I have 6 years of interpreting and translating experience. I got my start in medical interpreting but after graduating with a Spanish degree I pursued registration with The Supreme Court of Florida. I worked as a legal interpreter in the Orange County court system for a year. Together we’re going to learn how to become an interpreter.

How do I use this website?

My website is intended to be a resource center. Someplace you can always go back to to find how to be a better interpreter, how to grow your professional network, what certifications to pursue, and how to be good at your job!
